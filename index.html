The synopsis

This is an ABC television series that I fell in love with even before the first episode back in 2017. The The Good Doctor tells the story of a highly skilled young surgeon who has his residency at St Bon Aventure Hospital. His intellect and ability far outweigh his ability to adapt to his surroundings and mannerisms associated with a high functioning spectrum of Autism. The way his brain is wired portrays so intricately for the viewers as Dr. Murphy's thoughts, perceptions, and know-how play out before our very eyes. Dr. Murphy is considered a savant and though it's never specifically mentioned, quite possibly has Asperger Syndrone on the Autism Spectrum. Over the course of 6 seasons, Shaun has proven his abilities time and time again so much so that he is truly a valuable asset to the team.
